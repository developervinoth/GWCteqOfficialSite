What is Domo?

DOMO is a cloud-based business intelligence tool for data representation, visualization, warehousing, and producing key insights for decision-making and management. It is a SaaS platform that strives to transform huge amounts of raw data from databases and repositories into visualization tools like graphs, reports, and others.
It creates focused dashboards for better business data analysis, interpretation, integration, and utilization. These key insights form the basis for various organizational decisions on revenue, growth strategies, business goals, and opportunities.
DOMO provides a complete overview of critical data and actionable information to your teams and management, enabling them to streamline the workflow. The platform accumulates and integrates raw data from multiple sources to provide a comprehensive view of the overall business performance through real-time key performance indicators (KPIs) and metrics.
